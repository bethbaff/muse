meet the musers

b. has worked in finance, politics, healthcare, policy, and business across the social, public, and private sectors and has always craved a way to provide color commentary on the world around her in a private forum (without getting caught). b. musings allows her to write honestly and openly about her observations - and strong P.O.V. across a wide range of topics that don't fit into any traditional boxes.

b. has worked in international development operations, journalism (Ugandan Journalist of the Year, ahem), climate change policy, political organizing, political communications, and policy analysis. she joined b. musings to shout (loudly) to the world about the importance of action, policy, and regulation to curb the effects of climate change.

b. has worked in community and political organizing, digital marketing, and digital communications for large political organizations. she is a much sought after keynote and consultant for those trying to effectively blend on- and off-line organizing to achieve massive positive results. she joined b. musings to make fun of the people she works with.
